A. SCOPE

VR Chennai in its vision of becoming the world's leading shopping center company is moving towards digital transformation and has chosen Pathfinder as its preferred vendor to implement Retail Contract Management System (RCMS)

This system will help VR Chennai to establish transparency with concessionaires and provide intelligence for overall operational efficiency. 

As part of the RCMS, Pathfinder will implement an automated solution capturing Sales data which will be implemented. The information required to develop the interface is detailed in this document. 

We'd appreciate your cooperation to make this engagement successful

The Objectives of the solution are as follows:
* Design, develop & deploy POS PATROL - Integra & Print to capture sales data on a real-time basis or at agreed frequency
* During this process, Concessionaire shall extend the fullest cooperation and support to 'PATHFINDER' to achieve this Goal
* The developed agent will be tested at concessionaire's test environment, deployed at production environment and monitored for a period of 30 days for consistency & accuracy of data
We are confident this activity will greatly help our mutual interests, we look forward to your cooperation and support with VR Mall and PATHFINDER.

C. DEPLOYMENT OF POS PATROL | INTEGRA
1. APPROACH
* In this approach, agreed data parameters are extracted directly from the store's production database
* On Premise Database
o Provide Direct access to production database table & field information (or)
o Provide Database views of agreed data parameters with read only access at local production environment (or) 
o Share API documentation to access agreed data parameters with read only access.
* Off Premise Database (Subject to approval by Mall Commercial & IT)
o If the database is hosted outside the Mall premise at the head office or data center:
* Provide Direct access to production database table & field information (or)
* Provide Database views of agreed data parameters with read only access at production environment (or)
* Share API documentation to access agreed data parameters with read only access.
* Provide a WEB Service of agreed data parameters
* Once the inputs are shared, a service based utility will be developed exclusively for the brand by PATHFINDER and deployed at the store till or head office / data center which will automatically extracts data at agreed intervals, encrypts and pushes the file over a secure connection to the data staging server over local LAN or Internet for reporting.
